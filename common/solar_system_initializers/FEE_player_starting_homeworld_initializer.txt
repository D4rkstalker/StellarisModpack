#--		�	Should not compromise or corrupt your saves.

#--		�	This file allows users to select fallen empire homeworlds as player starting locations.
#--		�	Homeworlds are the capital systems of fallen empires.
#--		�	This file compiles all FE homeworlds and allow for their generation

#--		�	Empyral Sanctuary		FE Expanded 1.0		1x Gaia (25), 2x Gaia (20)
#--		�	Ethereal Bastion		FE Expanded 1.0		4x Ringworld
#--		�	Eternal Citadel			FE Expanded 1.0		2x Gaia (25)
#--		�	Earth					FE Expanded 1.0		1x Gaia (25), 1x Tropical (16), 1x Savannah (16), 1x Ocean (16), 1x Arctic (12)
#--		�	Terran Wasteland		FE Expanded 1.1		1x Gaia (25), 1x Tomb (25), 1x Desert (16), 1x Tomb (16), 1x Tundra (16), 1x Arctic (16)

#--		--------------------------- --------------------------- --------------------------- ---------------------------

#--
#--		IMPORTANT NOTE
#--		By providing players with Fallen Empire Homeworlds as starting location, two issues arises:
#--			�	Unintentional inbalance in favour of the user of FE homeworlds
#--			�	Conflict between a Fallen Empire's homeworld and the user's FE homeworld
#--
#--		Alleviation of issues:
#--			�	FE homeworld for players will not feature any colonies, auxiliaries, or ethos-unique systems
#--			�	Player-useable homeworlds will have a slightly different naming convention as to avoid confliction with their FE counterparts
#--			�	A future update may include a spawn blocker of the FE's homeworld that is being used by the player.
