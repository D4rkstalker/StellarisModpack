[b]This mod is designed to enhance the late game robomodding by adding more advanced versions of existing traits[/b]

Note: This mod makes robomodding a starting feature, therefore you do not need another mod that adds this feature.

[h1]Partner Mods[/h1]
- [url=http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1121692237]Gigastructural Engineering & More[/url] Gigastructure Related traits Require This Mod
- [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1390963071]Just More Traits[/url] Fixes for redundant Robotic traits and comp. is supported
