- This means if you want the vanilla POP Job checking mechanism work again, you don't have to exit the game and deactivate the MOD; you can just open the ModMenu and set it in Customisation menu. 
- What's more, you can also make the game to access the vanilla POP Job checking function in a low CPU resource occupancy status.


[h1]How it works?[/h1]
In short, when this MOD works, it makes vanilla daily POP check only works on those who has no job. Therefore, cpu occupacy by late game POP checking will be dramatically decreased.

[h1]Then, what's the drawback?[/h1]
- When [i]This mod will make your game 30% faster (maybe)[/i] is activated, First Production job(miner/energy) won't seek for acending into higher stratum (specialist). Only when unemployed population fill up all First Production job vacancy do they start to fill job vacancy of higher stratum. 
- This may cause the game losing a little reality, but in return you could get better performance.

[h1]Solution[/h1]
- Choose the appropriate POP Job checking density, and keep the balance between performance and gameplay.
--- For example, in the early game you can choose 
[b][i]High density - 6 days checking for all pops and extra 9 days checking for the unemployed per month[/i][/b]
--- in the late game when POPs become numerous, you can choose 
[b][i]Mid density - 3 days checking for all pops and extra 6 days checking for the unemployed per month[/i][/b]
[b][i]Low density - 1 days checking for all pops and extra 3 days checking for the unemployed per month[/i][/b]
[b][i]No checking - only 3 days checking for the unemployed per month[/i][/b]

[h1]如何发挥作用的？[/h1]
简而言之，发挥作用时它会使每日工作检测只作用于无业人口，这将极大减轻后期人口检测带来的巨大cpu占用。

[h1]那，缺点是什么呢？[/h1]
- 当[i]This mod will make your game 30% faster (maybe)[/i]开启时，一产人口(矿工，电工)不会追求进入更高阶层(专家)，无业人口会优先填满一产空缺，然后再填补二产职业空缺，游戏的真实性将有所下降。

[h1]解决方案[/h1]
- 选择合适的计算强度，保持流畅与游戏性的平衡。
---比如游戏初期，你可以选择高强度的人口检测 [b][i](每月中的6天检测全人口额外9天检测失业人口)[/i][/b]
---而随着人口总量的增多和产业的稳定，你可以逐步降低检测频率[b][i] (每月3天全人口，额外6天失业人口；每月1天全人口，额外3天失业人口；每个3天仅检测失业人口)[/i][/b]。
