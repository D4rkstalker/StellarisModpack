[h1] Stellaris v2.7.* Compatible [/h1]

[img]https://i.postimg.cc/yNgJTMGr/1.png[/img]
A year ago, there was a contest to make archaeology event mod in my community.
This mod is made as reward of the contest.

It adds 19 archaeology stories written by the community members.

Check out [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1999328266] Precursor Story Pack [/url] mod for 'Mod Making Phase 5' stories.

It currently provides following stories and relics:

[img]https://i.postimg.cc/GpfrVtn1/1-1.png[/img]
[h1] Mod Making Phase 1 [/h1]
- When the World sings
- A Bedroom Planet
- The First Music
- Dawn star is coming
- Ship of Theseus

[h1] Mod Making Phase 2 [/h1]
- Soul of the Art
- The Place where history has preserved (Displayed as: Kessler Syndrome)
- Geocentrism (Displayed as: Fixed World) (Include: Colossus Weapon, Geo-centralizer)
- Haunting Spectre (Displayed as: Ruined World)
- Rain World

[h1] Mod Making Phase 3 [/h1]
- Biggest Show in the World
- Axis of the Planet (Include: Multi Ends)
- Tomb of Space Whales
- King of the Underground City
- Strangely Fertile World (Leads to: Strangely Fertile World -> Awakened Moon)

[h1] Mod Making Phase 4 [/h1]
- Red Tomb World
- Beyond Earth: Purity (Displayed as: Desolate World) (Leads to: Desolate World -> Isolated World)
- Epilogue (Leads to: Ancient Probe -> Epilogue)
- Parasite Machine

[h1] Mod Making Phase 5 [/h1]
- [Precursor] The Sless Alliance
- [Precursor] Egalitarian Fallen Empire, The Coprean Constitution
- [Precursor] The Astanine

These 3 precursor stories are not included in this mod, check out [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1999328266] Precursor Story Pack [/url] mod for them.
